Steps to getting an e-bike

E-Bike 2022-08-31

This is my shortlisted choice: the Commuter S by Cleverley. I’m after a mid-drive (Bafang) motor, step-through (like a girl’s bike) with a decent range (60-95km).

I’m currently not in a state to do a test drive, but I do hope that will resolve. Situation is: I’m retired, have an 120 that’s truly excess to retirement, actually own a bicycle but find the hills a bit too much for my 70-y-o self. TheCommuter S Step-through absolutely looks like what I’m after - ticks all the boxes.
